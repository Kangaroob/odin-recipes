Blueberry Smoothie

Description

This refreshing smoothie will nourish your body and rejuvinate your spirit

Ingredients

  • Blueberries, frozen
  • Banana
  • Orange juice
  • A blender

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Put Blueberries, Banana, and Orange Juice into the Blender
  3. Blend it up! Add more OJ if it is hard to blend. Add more Blueberries if you want it thicker.
  4. Enjoy!
