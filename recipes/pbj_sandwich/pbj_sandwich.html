PBJ Sandwich

Description

An indulgent experience of jelly comingling with peanut butter, gripped in the embrace of bread

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • A Butter Knife

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients
  2. Use your butter knife to spread peanut butter on one side of one slice of bread
  3. Clean your butter knife (not with your tongue)
  4. Use your butter knife to spread jelly on one side of the other slice of bread
  5. Unite the peanut butter side of the first slice of bread with the jelly side of the other slice of bread
  6. Enjoy!
